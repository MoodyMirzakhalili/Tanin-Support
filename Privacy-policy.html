Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2, 2025

Introduction

PersonalizedDietPlanner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Height, weight, age, biological sex, activity level
  • Health Goals: Weight goals, fitness objectives, dietary preferences
  • Meal Data: Meals you log, custom recipes, meal plans
  • Nutrition Data: Calorie and nutrient intake, water consumption
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, preferences

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: App features used, session duration, crash reports
  • Technical Data: IP address, API request logs (for error handling)

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Sign In: If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID email (or private relay email)
  • Authentication: OAuth 2.1 authentication tokens (stored securely)

How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Nutrition Tracking: Calculate and display your daily nutrition intake
  • Meal Planning: Generate personalized meal plans based on your goals
  • Progress Monitoring: Track your health and fitness progress over time
  • Account Management: Create and manage your user account
  • Authentication: Secure login and session management

Service Improvement

  • AI Analysis: Use Claude AI to analyze meals and provide nutritional insights
  • Optimization: Improve meal planning algorithms and recommendations
  • Bug Fixing: Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Performance: Optimize app performance and user experience

Communication

  • Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Notifications: Send you important updates about your account or the app (with your consent)

Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted over the network uses TLS/HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication: OAuth 2.1 + PKCE for secure authentication
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using SHA-256, HKDF, and bcrypt
  • Token Management: Secure token storage and automatic rotation
  • Keychain Storage: Sensitive data stored in iOS Keychain Services
  • Database Security: PostgreSQL database with secure access controls

Data Location

  • Backend Servers: Your data is stored on secure cloud servers
  • Local Storage: Some data is cached locally using SwiftData for offline access
  • Data Sync: Data is synchronized between your device and our servers

Data Retention

  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active
  • You can request data deletion at any time (see "Your Rights" below)
  • Deleted data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  1. Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics)
    • These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data
    • They may only use your data to provide services to us
  2. AI Services: With Anthropic (Claude AI) for meal analysis
    • Meal descriptions and ingredients are sent for nutritional analysis
    • No personally identifiable information is included in AI requests
    • AI responses are cached to minimize data sharing
  3. Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
    • We will notify you when legally permitted
  4. Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
    • You will be notified of any change in ownership

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information in the app
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Export your data in a portable format (GDPR compliant)
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics or notifications (in app settings)

Account Deletion

To delete your account:

  1. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  2. Confirm deletion
  3. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Data Export

To export your data:

  1. Go to Profile → Settings → Export Data
  2. Your data will be provided in JSON format
